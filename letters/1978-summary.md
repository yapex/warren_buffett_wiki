# 1978 年致股东信摘要 / 1978 Letter Summary

## 概览 / Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1978 年伯克希尔经营收益（不含资本利得）为期初股东投资的 19.4%，接近 1972 年的历史纪录。与多元零售公司合并后，对蓝筹印花的持股提升至约 58%。过去三年每股净资产几乎翻番，年复合增长率约 25%。保险承保利润仍然强劲但周期已开始转向，股票投资组合三年间实现约 1.12 亿美元税前利得。巴菲特深入阐述了集中投资策略、少数股权的留存收益价值，以及对"不控制反而更好"管理哲学的进一步论证。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Berkshire's 1978 operating earnings (excluding capital gains) were 19.4% of beginning shareholders' equity, near the 1972 record. The merger with Diversified Retailing raised Blue Chip Stamps ownership to approximately 58%. Over three years, per-share book value nearly doubled at about 25% annually. Insurance underwriting remained strong but the cycle was turning. The stock portfolio generated approximately $112 million in pre-tax gains over three years. Buffett elaborated on concentrated investing, the value of retained earnings from minority interests, and further defended the "better management through non-control" philosophy.

## 核心主题 / Key Themes

1. **合并与会计** — 与多元零售合并后持股蓝筹印花 58%，全面合并报表掩盖了经济实质
2. **19.4% 股本回报率** — 接近历史纪录，但明确表示不可持续
3. **保险周期转向** — 预测 1979 年行业综合比率上升，费率增速（3%）远落后于成本增速（9%+）
4. **集中投资策略** — 明确提出"Our policy is to concentrate holdings"
5. **少数股权的留存收益** — 持股公司的留存收益对股东的长期意义可能等同于分配到的股利
6. **不控制反而更好** — SAFECO 案例：被动持有优秀管理层 > 主动控制平庸管理层
7. **买比建容易** — "easier to buy [a good insurance business] than create one"

## 精选原文 / Notable Excerpts

> [!quote] 集中投资 / Concentration Policy
> 我们的策略是集中持股。当我们对某项业务或其价格只是兴趣平平时，我们尽量不去东买一点、西买一点。当我们确信投资具有吸引力时，我们相信应该大手笔买入。
>
> Our policy is to concentrate holdings. We try to avoid buying a little of this or that when we are only lukewarm about the business or its price. When we are convinced as to attractiveness, we believe in buying worthwhile amounts.

> [!quote] 留存收益的价值 / Value of Retained Earnings
> SAFECO 的留存收益最终给股东创造的价值，很可能超过一元对一元。当我们的全资子公司能够以有吸引力的回报率在内部运用资金时，我们一点也不介意它们保留全部收益。同样的道理，对于我们持有少数股权的公司——如果其业绩记录显示资本的盈利运用前景甚至更好——我们又何必对它们保留收益感到不同呢？
>
> SAFECO's retained earnings may well eventually have a value to shareholders greater than 100 cents on the dollar. We are not at all unhappy when our wholly-owned businesses retain all of their earnings if they can utilize internally those funds at attractive rates. Why should we feel differently about retention of earnings by companies in which we hold small equity interests, but where the record indicates even better prospects for profitable employment of capital?

> [!quote] 纺织业的教训 / Textile Industry Lesson
> 纺织业以教科书般的方式说明了一个道理：在资本密集型行业中，生产相对无差异化产品的企业，除非遇到供给紧张或真正短缺的情况，否则注定只能赚取不足够的回报。只要产能过剩的状况存在，价格就会趋于反映直接运营成本而非投入的资本。
>
> The textile industry illustrates in textbook style how producers of relatively undifferentiated goods in capital intensive businesses must earn inadequate returns except under conditions of tight supply or real shortage. As long as excess productive capacity exists, prices tend to reflect direct operating costs rather than capital employed.

> [!quote] 买比建容易 / Buying vs. Building
> 收购一家好的保险公司不容易，但我们的经验是，买一家比自己建一家更容易。不过，我们会继续两条路都试，因为在这个领域取得成功的回报可以是非凡的。
>
> It is not easy to buy a good insurance business, but our experience has been that it is easier to buy one than create one. However, we will continue to try both approaches, since the rewards for success in this field can be exceptional.

> [!quote] 像老板一样思考 / Think Like Owners
> 与一群每天早上都盼着来上班、到了公司就本能地、精准地像老板一样思考问题的管理者共事，实在是一种乐趣。我们与一些最出色的人站在了一起。
>
> It is a real pleasure to work with managers who enjoy coming to work each morning and, once there, instinctively and unerringly think like owners. We are associated with some of the very best.
