# 1989年致股东信摘要 / 1989 Letter Summary

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 信件概述
1989年信是巴菲特经典之作，首次系统回顾了管理伯克希尔25年来的失误与教训。当年伯克希尔净值增长15.15亿美元（44.4%），每股账面价值从19.46美元增至4,296.01美元，年复合增长率23.8%。信中首次明确提出"透视收益"（look-through earnings）概念，并详细讨论了零息债券的滥用问题。此外，1989年伯克希尔大幅增持可口可乐（从1,417万股增至2,335万股），完成了对波仙珠宝的收购。

### 核心主题
- **内在价值 vs 账面价值**：内在价值已持续超过账面价值增速；但过去"双重收益"（企业价值增长+市场重估）的时代已结束，未来只能依赖"单一收益"
- **递延税的本质**：递延税类似于来自财政部的无息贷款，Rip Van Winkle式长期持有在数学上有显著优势
- **透视收益**：伯克希尔的真正盈利能力应包含被投资企业的留存收益，而不仅仅是分红
- **保险业分析**：行业产能过剩导致承保利润持续恶化；伯克希尔在飓风后迅速提供2.5亿美元巨灾再保险
- **零息债券的批判**：EBDIT指标和零息债券被滥用于为低质量收购融资，本质是"金融炼金术"
- **25年失误总结**：烟蒂投资法的教训、制度惯性的发现、与优秀的人合作的重要性
- **收购标准**：重申六大收购标准，偏好简单、低负债、高ROE的企业

### 重要数据
- 净值增长：15.15亿美元（+44.4%）
- 每股账面价值：$19.46 → $4,296.01（25年复合23.8%）
- 递延税负债：超11亿美元
- 可口可乐持股：14,172,500 → 23,350,000股
- 新增投资：吉列6亿美元可转换优先股、全美航空3.58亿美元、冠军国际3亿美元
- 零息可转换债券发行：9.026亿美元，15年期

## 🇺🇸 English

### Letter Overview
The 1989 letter is a Buffett classic, featuring the first systematic review of mistakes from 25 years of managing Berkshire. Net worth grew $1.515 billion (44.4%), with per-share book value compounding at 23.8% annually from $19.46 to $4,296.01. The letter introduces "look-through earnings" and delivers a devastating critique of zero-coupon bond abuse. Berkshire also dramatically increased its Coca-Cola holdings and acquired Borsheim's.

### Key Themes
- **Intrinsic Value vs Book Value**: Intrinsic value growth has exceeded book value; but the era of "double-dip" returns (business value growth + market revaluation) is over
- **Deferred Taxes as Interest-Free Loan**: Long-term "Rip Van Winkle" investing has a mathematical edge due to tax deferral
- **Look-Through Earnings**: True earning power includes retained earnings of investees
- **Insurance Industry**: Overcapacity continues to deteriorate underwriting results; Berkshire swiftly provided $250M catastrophe coverage after Hugo
- **Zero-Coupon Bond Critique**: EBDIT metrics and zeros used to finance junk acquisitions = "financial alchemy"
- **25-Year Mistakes**: Cigar-butt investing lessons, discovery of the institutional imperative, importance of working with admirable people

### Key Figures
- Net worth gain: $1.515B (+44.4%)
- Per-share book value: $19.46 → $4,296.01 (25yr CAGR 23.8%)
- Coca-Cola shares: 14.17M → 23.35M
- New investments: Gillette $600M, USAir $358M, Champion $300M convertible preferreds
- Zero-coupon convertible debentures issued: $902.6M
