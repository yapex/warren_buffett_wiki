# 留存收益 / Retained Earnings

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 留存收益是指公司净利润中不作为股利分配给股东、而是保留在公司内部用于再投资的部分。巴菲特在 1978 年股东信中提出了一个重要洞见：对于少数股权持有的优秀公司，其留存收益虽然不直接体现在伯克希尔的报告收益中，但对股东的长期价值可能超过一元对一元——前提是管理层能够以有吸引力的回报率运用这些资金。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Retained earnings are the portion of net income that a company keeps for reinvestment rather than distributing as dividends. In the 1978 letter, Buffett offered a key insight: for minority-held outstanding companies, retained earnings - while not reflected in Berkshire's reported earnings - may ultimately create shareholder value greater than 100 cents on the dollar, provided management can deploy the capital at attractive rates of return.

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1. **全资子公司留存收益**：如果公司能以有吸引力的回报率在内部运用资金，保留全部收益是合理的
> 2. **少数股权留存收益**：对持股的优秀公司，其留存收益的意义与分配到的股利同样真实
> 3. **一元对一元**：优秀公司的留存收益最终可能给股东创造超过 100% 的价值
> 4. **反向命题**：如果行业资本需求低，或管理层有将资金投入低回报项目的记录，则应分配股利或回购股票
> 5. **不体现在报告中**：少数股权公司的留存收益不进入伯克希尔的经营收益，但长期意义同样重大

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> 1. **Wholly-owned subsidiaries**: If they can use funds internally at attractive rates, retaining all earnings is rational
> 2. **Minority interests**: Retained earnings of portfolio companies are just as real as dividends received
> 3. **100 cents on the dollar**: Well-run companies' retained earnings may eventually exceed dollar-for-dollar value
> 4. **Reverse proposition**: In low capital requirement industries, or with poor capital allocators, earnings should be paid out or used for share repurchases
> 5. **Not in reported earnings**: Doesn't enter operating earnings but has equal long-term significance

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> **SAFECO 案例**：1978 年伯克希尔持有的 SAFECO 股份对应收益约 $6.1M，但仅约 18%（~$1.1M）以股利形式体现在经营收益中。巴菲特认为其余 82% 的留存收益虽然不可报告，但同样真实。SAFECO 的管理层能够将这些资金有利地运用，因此留存收益最终给股东创造的价值很可能超过一元对一元。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> **SAFECO Case**: In 1978, Berkshire's share of SAFECO earnings was about $6.1M, but only about 18% (~$1.1M) was reflected in operating earnings as dividends. Buffett argued the remaining 82% in retained earnings, while unreported, was just as real. Since SAFECO's management could employ the funds advantageously, retained earnings would likely eventually create value exceeding 100 cents on the dollar.

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] 留存收益的真实价值
> 我们相信其余部分虽然无法在报告中体现，但对我们最终获益的意义与分配到手的那部分完全一样。实际上，SAFECO 的留存收益最终给股东创造的价值，很可能超过一元对一元。
>
> We believe the balance, although not reportable, to be just as real in terms of eventual benefit to us as the amount distributed. In fact, SAFECO's retained earnings may well eventually have a value to shareholders greater than 100 cents on the dollar.
> — [1978-letter](../letters/1978-letter.md)

> [!quote] 全资子公司 vs 少数股权
> 当我们的全资子公司能够以有吸引力的回报率在内部运用资金时，我们一点也不介意它们保留全部收益。同样的道理，对于我们持有少数股权的公司——如果其业绩记录显示资本的盈利运用前景甚至更好——我们又何必对它们保留收益感到不同呢？
>
> We are not at all unhappy when our wholly-owned businesses retain all of their earnings if they can utilize internally those funds at attractive rates. Why should we feel differently about retention of earnings by companies in which we hold small equity interests, but where the record indicates even better prospects for profitable employment of capital?
> — [1978-letter](../letters/1978-letter.md)

> [!quote] 反向命题
> 如果某些行业对资本需求很低，或者管理层有把资金投入低回报项目的记录，那么收益就应该分配给股东或用来回购股票——后者往往是资本运用中最有吸引力的选择。
>
> In industries with low capital requirements, or if management has a record of plowing capital into projects of low profitability; then earnings should be paid out or used to repurchase shares - often by far the most attractive option for capital utilization.
> — [1978-letter](../letters/1978-letter.md)
