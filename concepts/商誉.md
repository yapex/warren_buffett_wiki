# 商誉 / Goodwill

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 定义
商誉是当企业收购价格超过所收购可辨认净资产公允价值时的差额。在会计处理中，这笔差额被计入"超出所收购净资产权益的成本"科目，简称商誉。

### 两种商誉
1. **会计商誉**：收购时产生的会计记录，需要在不超过40年内摊销
2. **[经济商誉](经济商誉.md)**：企业超额盈利能力的资本化价值，是真正重要的概念

### 巴菲特的关键洞见
巴菲特在1983年信附录中详细阐述了商誉问题：
- 会计商誉的摊销不是真正的经济成本
- [喜诗糖果](companies/喜诗糖果.md)的会计商誉在减少，但经济商誉在增长
- 通货膨胀对商誉的影响：拥有大量经济商誉的企业反而受益

### 巴菲特原话
> "在分析经营成果时——即评估一个业务单元的内在经济效益时——摊销费用应当被忽略。一家企业在无杠杆的净有形资产上预期能赚取多少，是衡量该业务经济吸引力的最佳指南。"
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)

## 🇺🇸 English

### Definition
Goodwill is created when an acquisition price exceeds the fair value of identifiable net assets acquired. In accounting, this difference is recorded as an asset called "excess of cost over equity in net assets acquired."

### Two Types of Goodwill
1. **Accounting Goodwill**: Created at acquisition, must be amortized over up to 40 years
2. **[Economic Goodwill](经济商誉.md)**: The capitalized value of a business's excess earning power

### Buffett's Key Insight
In the 1983 letter appendix, Buffett explained:
- Accounting goodwill amortization is not a true economic cost
- [See's](companies/喜诗糖果.md) accounting goodwill decreases while economic goodwill grows
- Inflation's effect on goodwill: businesses with significant economic goodwill actually benefit

### Key Quote
> "In analysis of operating results...amortization charges should be ignored. What a business can be expected to earn on unleveraged net tangible assets is the best guide to the economic attractiveness of the operation."
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)
