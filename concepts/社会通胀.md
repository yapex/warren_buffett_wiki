# 社会通胀 / Social Inflation

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 社会通胀指社会和陪审团不断扩大保险保单理赔范围的倾向，独立于货币通胀之外，持续推高保险理赔成本。巴菲特在 1977 年指出，保险理赔成本每月增长约 1%，其中既有货币通胀因素，也有社会通胀因素。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Social inflation refers to the tendency of society and juries to broaden the definition of what insurance policies cover, driving up claim costs independently of monetary inflation. Buffett noted in 1977 that insurance costs rise at about 1% per month, driven by both monetary inflation and social inflation.

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 与货币通胀不同，社会通胀是文化和法律层面的变化
> - 表现为：陪审团裁决金额越来越大、保险覆盖范围被不断拓宽
> - 推动保险理赔成本每月增长约 1%
> - 意味着保险公司必须持续上调费率才能维持承保利润
> - 如果费率上调速度跟不上成本上涨，承保利润必然缩水

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Unlike monetary inflation, social inflation is a cultural and legal change
> - Manifested as: larger jury awards, broader interpretations of coverage
> - Drives insurance claim costs up about 1% per month
> - Insurers must continuously raise rates to maintain underwriting profits
> - If rate increases lag cost increases, underwriting profits must shrink

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 评估保险公司时，需同时关注货币通胀和社会通胀
> - 社会通胀难以量化，但对长期保险成本有重大影响
> - 承保纪律在社会通胀环境下更加重要

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - When evaluating insurers, monitor both monetary and social inflation
> - Social inflation is hard to quantify but materially impacts long-term insurance costs
> - Underwriting discipline becomes even more important under social inflation

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [[1977-letter]]
> 这既源于持续的货币通胀推高了人身伤害和财产修复的成本，也源于"社会通胀"——社会和陪审团对保单理赔范围的界定不断扩大。
>
> This is due to continuous monetary inflation affecting the cost of repairing humans and property, as well as "social inflation", a broadening definition by society and juries of what is covered by insurance policies.
