# 通货膨胀 / Inflation

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 通货膨胀是指整体物价水平持续上涨，导致货币购买力下降的经济现象。巴菲特对通货膨胀有着深刻的洞察，特别关注其对企业价值、债券投资和资本配置的影响。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Inflation refers to the general increase in prices of goods and services over time, reducing the purchasing power of money. Buffett had deep insights into inflation, particularly regarding its impact on business value, bond investments, and capital allocation.

## 巴菲特的观点 / Buffett's Perspective

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 在1984年致股东信中，巴菲特深入讨论了通货膨胀对投资的影响。他指出：
>
> - 债券和美元一样"坚挺"，而他对美元的长期前景并不看好
> - 在通胀时期，拥有超凡经济特质的核心业务可以用少量增量资本获得极高的回报率
> - 大多数企业无法显著提高其平均股本回报率——即使在通胀环境下
> - 持有大量有形资产的企业通常只能赚取很低的回报率
> - 拥有持久无形资产的企业在通胀时期更具优势

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> In the 1984 letter, Buffett discussed the impact of inflation on investing:
>
> - Bonds are "as sound as a dollar", and he viewed the long-term outlook for dollars as dismal
> - During inflationary periods, companies with extraordinary economic characteristics can earn very high returns on small amounts of incremental capital
> - Most businesses cannot significantly improve their average returns on equity - even under inflationary conditions
> - Companies with large amounts of tangible assets typically earn low rates of return
> - Businesses with durable intangible assets have advantages during inflationary periods

## 投资启示 / Investment Implications

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特认为，通货膨胀对投资者的影响包括：
>
> 1. **债券风险**：长期债券在通胀环境下会损失购买力
> 2. **企业价值**：需要更高水平的留存收益来维持实际利润
> 3. **资本配置**：资本密集型企业受通胀伤害最大
> 4. **无形资产**：拥有经济商誉的企业能更好地抵御通胀

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett believed that inflation affects investors in several ways:
>
> 1. **Bond risk**: Long-term bonds lose purchasing power in inflationary environments
> 2. **Business value**: Higher levels of retained earnings are needed to maintain real profits
> 3. **Capital allocation**: Capital-intensive businesses are hurt most by inflation
> 4. **Intangibles**: Businesses with economic goodwill can better withstand inflation
