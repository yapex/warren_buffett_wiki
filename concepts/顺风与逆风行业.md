# 顺风与逆风行业 / Businesses with Tailwinds vs Headwinds

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特用"顺风"（tailwinds）和"逆风"（headwinds）来描述不同行业的结构性特征。顺风行业即使管理层犯了一些错误，整体仍能取得不错业绩；逆风行业即使管理层非常优秀，也只能取得微薄利润。选择正确的行业比选择优秀的管理层更重要。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett uses "tailwinds" and "headwinds" to describe the structural characteristics of different industries. In tailwind industries, even management mistakes can coexist with satisfactory overall results; in headwind industries, even excellent management can only produce modest returns. Choosing the right industry matters more than choosing excellent management.

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 1977 年的保险业 vs 纺织业是经典对比
> - 保险业：犯过大错（保证责任险、迈阿密扩张、航空代出单），但整体仍相当成功
> - 纺织业：管理层努力且诚实，但行业结构性劣势使得业绩始终不佳
> - 这一教训巴菲特说"有时候还得重新学一遍"——暗示后来仍有违反此原则的案例
> - 本质上与后来"护城河"和"竞争优势"思想一脉相承

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Insurance vs textiles in 1977 is the classic contrast
> - Insurance: major mistakes made (surety, Miami expansion, aviation fronting), but overall quite successful
> - Textiles: diligent and honest management, yet structural disadvantages persist
> - Buffett says this lesson "sometimes must be re-learned" — implying later violations
> - Fundamentally connected to later "moat" and "competitive advantage" thinking

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 投资前先判断行业是否处于"顺风"
> - 不应仅因为优秀的管理层就投资结构性困难的行业
> - 识别顺风行业的特征：定价权、进入壁垒、低成本优势等
> - 警惕"逆风行业中的好管理层"陷阱

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Before investing, first assess whether the industry has tailwinds
> - Don't invest in structurally difficult industries just because of good management
> - Identify tailwind characteristics: pricing power, barriers to entry, cost advantages
> - Beware the trap of "good management in a headwind industry"

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [1977-letter](../letters/1977-letter.md)
> 保险行业是这样一门生意：即使犯了一些错误，整体上仍然可以取得相当不错的业绩。这恰恰与我们的纺织业务相反——纺织业即使管理层非常优秀，平均下来也只能取得微薄的利润。
>
> It is comforting to be in a business where some mistakes can be made and yet a quite satisfactory overall performance can be achieved. This is the opposite case from our textile business where even very good management probably can average only modest results.

> [!quote] [1977-letter](../letters/1977-letter.md)
> 选择在顺风行业而非逆风行业中经营——这是各位管理层学到的一个教训，遗憾的是有时候还得重新学一遍。
>
> The importance of being in businesses where tailwinds prevail rather than headwinds — one of the lessons your management has learned, and, unfortunately, sometimes re-learned.
