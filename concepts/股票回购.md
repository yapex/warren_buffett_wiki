# 股票回购 / Stock Repurchase

## 概念定义 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 股票回购是指公司用现金从市场上购回自己发行的股票。巴菲特认为，当公司股价远低于其内在商业价值时，回购是最能增加股东财富的方式。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Stock repurchase refers to a company buying back its own shares from the market. Buffett believes that when a company's stock trades far below its intrinsic business value, repurchases are the best way to enhance shareholder wealth.

## 巴菲特的观点 / Buffett's View

### 回购的两种好处 / Two Benefits

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> **显而易见的好处**：简单的算术——以远低于每股内在商业价值的价格进行大规模回购，立即以非常显著的方式增加每股价值。
>
> **微妙的好处**：管理层回购股票的行为表明他们真正关注股东财富，而非扩张自己的势力范围。投资者会提高对公司的预期，推动股价更贴近内在价值。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> **The obvious benefit**: Basic arithmetic - major repurchases at prices well below per-share intrinsic business value immediately and significantly increase that value.
>
> **The subtle benefit**: Management's willingness to repurchase signals they act in shareholders' interests rather than expand management's domain. This causes investors to upgrade their expectations, driving market prices closer to intrinsic value.

### 回购的条件 / Conditions for Repurchases

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特对回购的认可仅限于那些基于价格与价值关系作出的回购。当股价远低于内在价值时，回购是最有利的。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett's endorsement of repurchases is limited to those dictated by price/value relationships. When stock trades far below intrinsic value, repurchases are most advantageous.

### 批评"绿邮讹诈" / Criticism of Greenmail

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特强烈批评"绿邮讹诈"式回购：
> - 勒索者持股后威胁管理层回购
> - 管理层为了息事宁人用其他股东的钱买通勒索者
> - 真正的股东利益受损

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett strongly criticizes "greenmail" repurchases where:
> - An extortionist threatens management after acquiring shares
> - Management pays off the extortionist with other shareholders' money
> - True shareholders bear the cost

> [!quote] [1984-letter](../letters/1984-letter.md)
> 看到这一点，现有股东和潜在股东都会提高对公司未来回报的预期。这种向上修正反过来又推动市场价格更加贴近内在商业价值。

> [!quote] [1984-letter](../letters/1984-letter.md)
> 价格完全理性。投资者理应为一家由具有明确维护股东利益倾向的管理层所经营的企业，支付比那些由自私自利、各行其是的管理层所经营的企业更高的价格。

## 案例：GEICO、华盛顿邮报、通用食品 / Case Studies

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1984年，巴菲特提到GEICO、华盛顿邮报和通用食品三大重仓股都进行了大规模回购，这些回购为伯克希尔和其他股东带来了实质性收益。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> In 1984, Buffett noted that GEICO, Washington Post, and General Foods - three of Berkshire's largest holdings - all engaged in significant stock repurchases, which materially benefited Berkshire and other shareholders.
