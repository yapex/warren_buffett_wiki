# 综合比率 / Combined Ratio

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 综合比率是保险业的承保业绩核心指标，等于理赔支出加营业费用除以保费收入。比率低于 100% 表示承保盈利，高于 100% 表示承保亏损。巴菲特在 1977 年信中多次引用综合比率来评估各保险子公司的表现。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> The combined ratio is the core metric of insurance underwriting performance, calculated as incurred losses plus operating expenses divided by premiums written. A ratio below 100% indicates underwriting profit; above 100% indicates underwriting loss. Buffett cited combined ratios extensively in the 1977 letter to evaluate each insurance subsidiary.

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 1977 年数据：再保险综合比率 107.1（亏损，但趋势在下降）；住宅保险公司均低于 100（盈利），内布拉斯加意外险以 93.8 领跑
> - 综合比率低于 100 = 承保盈利 + 免费使用浮存金（双倍好处）
> - 综合比率高于 100 = 承保亏损，但仍可能通过投资收益弥补
> - 巴菲特更看重综合比率的趋势而非单一年度的数字

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - 1977 data: Reinsurance combined ratio 107.1 (loss, but declining trend); Homestate companies all below 100 (profitable), Cornhusker led at 93.8
> - Combined ratio below 100 = underwriting profit + free use of float (double benefit)
> - Combined ratio above 100 = underwriting loss, but may be offset by investment income
> - Buffett focuses more on the trend of combined ratios than single-year figures

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [[1977-letter]]
> 虽然107.1的综合比率仍然不理想，但全年趋势是在持续下降。
>
> Although the combined ratio of 107.1 was unsatisfactory, its trend was downward throughout the year.
