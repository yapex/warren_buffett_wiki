# 困境反转 / Turnaround

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> "困境反转"（Turnaround）指投资于陷入经营困境、以远低于账面价值的价格出售的公司，期望其业务能够恢复盈利。巴菲特在 1979 年股东信中明确否定了这种投资策略，基于伯克希尔在纺织业和 Waumbec 纺织厂收购中的亲身经历，得出结论："困境反转很少真的能反转"。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> "Turnaround" refers to investing in troubled companies selling at deep discounts to book value, expecting their businesses to recover profitability. In the 1979 letter, Buffett explicitly rejected this strategy based on Berkshire's own experience in textiles and the Waumbec Mills acquisition, concluding: "turnarounds seldom turn."

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - "困境反转很少真的能反转"——无论是经营还是投资经验都得出了这个结论
> - 同样的精力和才华，花在以合理价格买入好公司上，远比花在以便宜价格买入烂公司上要值得
> - Waumbec 纺织厂的教训：买入价格远低于营运资金，大量设备等于白送，但仍然是个错误
> - 旧问题刚按下去，新问题又冒出来
> - 纺织业务产生一些现金，但相对于投入资本回报偏低——这不是管理层的问题，而是行业的问题
> - "轻松的路"可能反而是更明智的选择：以高价买入有线电视等顺风行业的公司

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - "Turnarounds seldom turn" — both operating and investment experience led to this conclusion
> - The same energies and talent are much better employed in a good business at a fair price than in a poor business at a bargain price
> - Waumbec Mills lesson: purchased well below working capital, got machinery and real estate for less than nothing — still a mistake
> - New problems arose as fast as old problems were tamed
> - Textile business produces some cash but at a low rate compared to capital — not a management issue, but an industry issue
> - The "easy" route may be wiser: buying into tailwind businesses like network TV at premium prices

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 在评估"便宜"公司时，首先判断行业是否处于结构性逆风
> - 区分"好公司遇到暂时困难"和"烂公司本来就是烂公司"
> - 哪怕买入价格极具吸引力，行业根本性问题也不会因为便宜而消失
> - Waumbec 的收购并非灾难——部分业务成为新贝德福德室内装饰品生产线的有益补充——但"最初的收购理由并没有得到验证"

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - When evaluating "cheap" companies, first assess whether the industry faces structural headwinds
> - Distinguish between "good companies with temporary problems" and "bad companies that are simply bad"
> - Even at extremely attractive prices, fundamental industry problems don't disappear
> - The Waumbec acquisition wasn't a disaster — certain portions proved valuable additions — but "our original rationale did not prove out"

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [1979-letter](../letters/1979-letter.md)
> 无论是经营还是投资的经验都让我们得出一个结论：所谓的"困境反转"很少真的能反转，同样的精力和才华，花在以合理价格买入好公司上，远比花在以便宜价格买入烂公司上要值得。
>
> Both our operating and investment experience cause us to conclude that "turnarounds" seldom turn, and that the same energies and talent are much better employed in a good business purchased at a fair price than in a poor business purchased at a bargain price.

> [!quote] [1979-letter](../letters/1979-letter.md)
> 尽管标价不菲，但走这条"轻松"的路可能反而是更明智的选择。
>
> Despite a fancy price tag, the "easy" business may be the better route to go.
