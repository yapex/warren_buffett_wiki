# 集中投资 / Concentrated Investing

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 集中投资是指将资金集中投入到少数经过深入研究的、高确信度的投资标的中，而非广泛分散到大量平庸的机会里。巴菲特在 1978 年股东信中首次明确将"集中持股"表述为伯克希尔的正式策略。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Concentrated investing means committing capital to a small number of deeply researched, high-conviction investments rather than spreading it broadly across many mediocre opportunities. Buffett first explicitly stated "concentrate holdings" as Berkshire's formal policy in the 1978 shareholder letter.

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1. **不感兴趣就不买**：当对某项业务或价格只是兴趣平平，不去东买一点西买一点
> 2. **确信时大手笔**：当确信投资具有吸引力时，应该大手笔买入
> 3. **四个条件全部满足才出手**：(1) 能理解 (2) 长期前景好 (3) 诚实能干的管理层 (4) 价格有吸引力
> 4. **宁可错过也不将就**：条件 (4) 价格吸引力往往是最难满足的

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> 1. **Don't dabble**: Avoid buying small amounts of things you are only lukewarm about
> 2. **Buy meaningful amounts**: When convinced of attractiveness, commit worthwhile capital
> 3. **All four criteria must be met**: (1) understandable (2) favorable long-term prospects (3) honest & competent management (4) attractive price
> 4. **Patience over compromise**: Criterion (4) - attractive price - is often the hardest to satisfy

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1978 年底，伯克希尔保险投资组合中市值超过 $8M 的持仓仅有 8 只股票，但总市值达到 $163.9M（成本 $94.3M）。其中 [[华盛顿邮报]] 单一持仓市值 $43.4M，占总持仓的 26.5%。[[盖可保险]]（普通股+可转换优先股合计）市值 $37.4M，占 22.8%。这种高度集中的策略使伯克希尔在道琼斯指数从 852 跌到 805 的三年间，实现了约 $112M 的税前投资利得。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> At year-end 1978, Berkshire's insurance portfolio held only 8 positions with market values exceeding $8M, but their combined value reached $163.9M (cost $94.3M). [[华盛顿邮报]] alone accounted for $43.4M (26.5% of the portfolio). [[盖可保险]] (common + convertible preferred combined) totaled $37.4M (22.8%). This high concentration enabled Berkshire to achieve approximately $112M in pre-tax investment gains over three years while the Dow fell from 852 to 805.

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] 正式政策声明
> 我们的策略是集中持股。当我们对某项业务或其价格只是兴趣平平时，我们尽量不去东买一点、西买一点。当我们确信投资具有吸引力时，我们相信应该大手笔买入。
>
> Our policy is to concentrate holdings. We try to avoid buying a little of this or that when we are only lukewarm about the business or its price. When we are convinced as to attractiveness, we believe in buying worthwhile amounts.
> — [[1978-letter]]

## 思想演变 / Evolution of Thought

| 年份 | 发展 |
|------|------|
