# 特许经营权 / Franchise

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 定义
消费者特许经营权是指消费者基于与产品和员工无数次愉快体验而建立的深厚好感，使得企业能够以超出生产成本的价格销售产品。

### 特许经营权的特征
1. 消费者愿意为品牌支付溢价
2. 竞争对手难以复制
3. 能够抵御通货膨胀的影响
4. 是[经济商誉](经济商誉.md)的首要来源

### 巴菲特的例子
- **喜诗糖果**：消费者宁愿支付2-3倍价格购买喜诗，而非其他品牌
- **布法罗新闻报**：渗透率全美最高，读者忠诚度极高
- **内布拉斯加家具商场**：采购卓越、费用率低、价格优势明显

### 特许经营权的来源
1. **消费特许经营权**：消费者基于品牌好感
2. **政府特许经营权**：如电视台（不受利润管制）
3. **低成本生产者地位**：持久的竞争优势

### 巴菲特原话
> "这种声誉创造了一种消费者特许经营权，使得决定销售价格的主要因素是产品对买家的价值，而非生产成本。消费者特许经营权是经济商誉的首要来源。"
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)

## 🇺🇸 English

### Definition
Consumer franchise is the deep goodwill established through countless pleasant experiences with a product and personnel, allowing a business to sell products at prices above production cost.

### Characteristics
1. Consumers willing to pay premium for brand
2. Difficult for competitors to replicate
3. Resilient against inflation
4. Primary source of [economic goodwill](经济商誉.md)

### Buffett's Examples
- **See's**: Consumers prefer See's at 2-3x the price of competitors
- **Buffalo News**: Highest penetration ratio in US, extreme reader loyalty
- **Nebraska Furniture Mart**: Brilliant buying, ultra-low expense ratios

### Sources of Franchise
1. **Consumer franchises**: Based on brand goodwill
2. **Government franchises**: Like TV stations (not subject to profit regulation)
3. **Enduring low-cost position**: Sustainable competitive advantage

### Key Quote
> "Such a reputation creates a consumer franchise that allows the value of the product to the purchaser, rather than its production cost, to be the major determinant of selling price. Consumer franchises are a prime source of economic Goodwill."
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)
