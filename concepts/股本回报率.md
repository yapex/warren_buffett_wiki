# 股本回报率 / Return on Equity (ROE)

## 概念解析 / Definition

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 股本回报率（Return on Equity, ROE）是衡量企业利用股东权益创造利润能力的指标，计算方式为净利润除以期初股东权益。巴菲特认为，ROE 是评估管理层经济表现的最合理指标，远比"每股收益创新高"更有意义。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Return on Equity measures a company's ability to generate profits from shareholders' equity, calculated as net income divided by beginning equity. Buffett considers ROE the most appropriate measure of managerial economic performance, far more meaningful than "record earnings per share."

## 核心要义 / Key Principles

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 批评"每股收益创纪录"的误导性：企业每年都在增加股本基础，即使业绩平平，EPS 也会因股本增加而"被动"增长
> - 类比：一个不动的定期存款账户，靠复利也能每年产生越来越多的利息
> - ROE 剔除了股本规模扩大的影响，反映管理层真实的经济绩效
> - 1977 年伯克希尔的 ROE 为 19%，高于自身长期平均和美国企业整体水平
> - 但巴菲特也指出，即便 EPS 增长 37%，由于期初资本也增加了 24%，实际表现并不像表面那么亮眼

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Critiques the misleading nature of "record EPS": businesses customarily add to their equity base, so even mediocre performance can produce rising EPS
> - Analogy: a dormant savings account produces steadily rising interest through compounding
> - ROE strips out the effect of equity expansion, revealing true managerial performance
> - Berkshire's 1977 ROE was 19%, above both its own long-term average and American industry's
> - Even with 37% EPS growth, beginning capital also rose 24%, making the gain less impressive

## 实践应用 / Practical Application

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 投资分析时，优先关注 ROE 而非 EPS 增长率
> - 将 ROE 与同行业、自身历史水平对比
> - 注意区分经营性收益和非经常性收益对 ROE 的影响
> - 结合债务水平看 ROE——高杠杆可以人为推高 ROE

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - When analyzing investments, prioritize ROE over EPS growth rate
> - Compare ROE against industry peers and the company's own history
> - Distinguish between operating and non-recurring items affecting ROE
> - Consider debt levels alongside ROE — high leverage can artificially inflate ROE

>
> Except for special cases, we believe a more appropriate measure of managerial economic performance to be return on equity capital.

>
> After all, even a totally dormant savings account will produce steadily rising interest earnings each year because of compounding.
