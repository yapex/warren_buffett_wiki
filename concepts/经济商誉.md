# 经济商誉 / Economic Goodwill

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 定义
当企业预期能从有形资产上获得大幅超过市场水平的回报率时，从逻辑上讲，企业的价值远远高于其净有形资产。这种超额回报的资本化价值就是经济商誉。

### 与会计商誉的区别
| 特征 | 经济商誉 | 会计商誉 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 产生方式 | 企业自身经营积累 | 收购时记录 |
| 变化趋势 | 通常随时间和通胀增长 | 每年摊销减少 |
| 真实性 | 真正的经济价值 | 会计处理 |
| 重要性 | 真正重要 | 可能误导 |

### 喜诗糖果案例
- 1972年收购时：净有形资产800万，收购价2500万，超出的1700万记为会计商誉
- 1983年：净有形资产约2000万，税后利润1300万
- 经济商誉 = 1300万/12% - 2000万 ≈ 8800万（远超会计商誉）

### 通货膨胀的影响
经济商誉往往随通胀按比例增长其名义价值：
- 企业需要增加有形资产投资来维持实际利润
- 商誉密集型企业所需有形投资少，通胀时更受益
- "在通胀时期，商誉是一件不断带来回报的礼物"

### 巴菲特原话
> "当企业预期能从有形资产上获得大幅超过市场水平的回报率时，从逻辑上讲，企业的价值远远高于其净有形资产。这种超额回报的资本化价值就是经济商誉。"
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)

## 🇺🇸 English

### Definition
When a business can be expected to produce earnings on net tangible assets considerably in excess of market rates of return, logically the business is worth far more than net tangible assets. The capitalized value of this excess return is economic goodwill.

### Sources of Economic Goodwill
1. **Consumer franchises**: Brand loyalty allowing premium pricing
2. **Government franchises**: Like TV stations not subject to profit regulation
3. **Enduring competitive advantages**: Low-cost producer position

### The See's Candy Example
- 1972 acquisition: $8M net tangible assets, $25M purchase price
- 1983: ~$20M net tangible assets, $13M after-tax profit
- Economic goodwill ≈ $13M/12% - $20M ≈ $88M (far exceeding accounting goodwill)

### Inflation Effects
Economic goodwill tends to rise proportionally with inflation:
- Businesses need more tangible assets to maintain real earnings
- Goodwill-intensive businesses need less tangible investment
- "During inflation, Goodwill is the gift that keeps giving"

### Key Quote
> "Businesses logically are worth far more than net tangible assets when they can be expected to produce earnings on such assets considerably in excess of market rates of return. The capitalized value of this excess return is economic Goodwill."
> — [1983-letter](letters/1983-letter.md)
