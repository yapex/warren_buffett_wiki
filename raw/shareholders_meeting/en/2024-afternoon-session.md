# 2024 Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting - Afternoon Session

**Source**: CNBC Warren Buffett Archive  
**Date**: May 6, 2024  
**Location**: Omaha, Nebraska  
**Duration**: ~1h57m  
**URL**: https://buffett.cnbc.com/video/2024/05/06/afternoon-session---2024-meeting.html

---

In the afternoon session , Warren Buffett reveals Berkshire has sold its entire stake in Paramount Global at a loss. He and Vice Chairman Greg Abel also cover the generosity of Berkshire shareholders, the fine points of drafting a will, and Abel's future role as the company's CEO after Buffett is no longer in charge.
