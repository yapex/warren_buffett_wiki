# 比尔·斯奈德 / Bill Snyder

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 比尔·斯奈德（Bill Snyder）与杰克·伯恩（Jack Byrne）共同管理[盖可保险](../companies/盖可保险.md)的核心保险业务。在他们的领导下，GEICO在低成本汽车和住宅保险方面建立了重大且可持续的竞争优势。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Bill Snyder, along with Jack Byrne, co-managed GEICO's core insurance business. Under their leadership, GEICO established a major, sustainable competitive advantage in low-cost auto and homeowners insurance.
