# 约翰·林沃尔特 / John Ringwalt

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 约翰·林沃尔特（John Ringwalt）负责伯克希尔的住宅保险（Homestate）业务集团。1978 年该集团整体表现令人失望，但巴菲特表示相信 John 有能力扭转局面。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> John Ringwalt was responsible for Berkshire's Homestate insurance operation group. The group's overall performance was disappointing in 1978, but Buffett expressed confidence in John's ability to correct the situation.
