# 弗洛伊德·泰勒 / Floyd Taylor

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 弗洛伊德·泰勒（Floyd Taylor）是堪萨斯火灾及意外险公司（Kansas Fire and Casualty）的管理者。在他的领导下，这家子公司在第一个完整经营年度取得了 1978 年住宅保险集团中最佳的损失赔付比率，被巴菲特评价为"一个真正了不起的开局"。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Floyd Taylor managed Kansas Fire and Casualty. Under his leadership, the subsidiary achieved the best loss ratio among the homestate companies in its first full year of business in 1978, described by Buffett as "a truly remarkable start."
