# 杰克·林沃尔特 / Jack Ringwalt

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 杰克·林沃尔特（Jack Ringwalt）是国民保险公司（National Indemnity Company）的创始人。1967 年巴菲特通过收购他创建的保险业务板块进入保险行业，这个板块至今仍是伯克希尔保险业务中最好的一块。杰克奠定的经营哲学至今仍深深刻印在公司身上。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Jack Ringwalt was the founder of National Indemnity Company. In 1967, Buffett entered the insurance business by purchasing the insurance segment that Ringwalt built, which remains by far the best portion of Berkshire's insurance business. The operating philosophy Ringwalt established remains etched upon the company.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特在 1967 年以约 $8.6M 收购了杰克·林沃尔特创建的国民保险业务，这笔收购被认为是伯克希尔历史上最重要的交易之一。巴菲特多次强调，后来所有的保险扩张努力都不及当初买下的这块业务。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett acquired Jack Ringwalt's National Indemnity insurance segment for approximately $8.6M in 1967, widely considered one of the most important transactions in Berkshire's history. Buffett has repeatedly noted that all subsequent insurance expansion efforts have not matched the original acquisition.
