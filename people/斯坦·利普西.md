# 斯坦·利普西 / Stan Lipsey

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 斯坦·利普西（Stan Lipsey）是[布法罗晚报](../companies/布法罗晚报.md)的出版人（Publisher），在报纸行业拥有卓越的管理能力。1984年，他领导下的布法罗晚报在成本控制方面取得了显著成效，不计编辑部工时的总工作时数减少了约2.8%，总体成本仅增长4.9%。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Stan Lipsey is the Publisher of the Buffalo Evening News, demonstrating exceptional management capabilities in the newspaper industry. In 1984, under his leadership, the Buffalo Evening News achieved remarkable cost control, with total hours worked (excluding newsroom) decreasing by about 2.8% and overall costs increasing only 4.9%.
