# 路易斯·文森蒂 / Louis Vincenti

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 路易斯·文森蒂（Louis Vincenti）是[[韦斯科金融公司]]的经理，该公司由[[蓝筹印花]]持股 80%。1977 年在巴菲特的信中被提及为韦斯科金融的负责人。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Louis Vincenti was the manager of Wesco Financial Corporation, 80% owned by Blue Chip Stamps. Mentioned in the 1977 letter as the head of Wesco Financial.
