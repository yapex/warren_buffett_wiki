# 吉恩·阿贝格 / Gene Abegg

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 吉恩·阿贝格（Gene Abegg）于 1931 年以 25 万美元创立了[伊利诺伊国民银行](../companies/伊利诺伊国民银行.md)。他以极高的资产回报率闻名——1977 年该银行的资产回报率约为大型银行的 3 倍。巴菲特 1969 年收购该行后，阿贝格从未增资，却累计支付了 2,000 万美元的现金股利。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Gene Abegg founded the Illinois National Bank in 1931 with $250,000. He was renowned for achieving extraordinarily high returns on assets — in 1977 the bank's return on assets was about three times that of most large banks. After Buffett's 1969 acquisition, Abegg never contributed additional capital, yet the bank paid $20 million in cumulative cash dividends.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 1969 年巴菲特收购伊利诺伊国民银行时，阿贝格继续留任
> - 80 岁仍"在一线经营着这家无人能及的银行"
> - 1977 年主动提出引入接班人，展现了卓越的管理者自我认知
> - 巴菲特称其经营着"无人能及"的银行

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Stayed on after Buffett's 1969 acquisition of Illinois National Bank
> - At age 80, still "running a banking operation without peer"
> - Proactively requested a successor in 1977, demonstrating exceptional self-awareness
> - Buffett described his operation as "without peer"
