# 弗兰克·德纳尔多 / Frank DeNardo

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 弗兰克·德纳尔多（Frank DeNardo）于 1978 年春季加入伯克希尔，负责整顿国民保险公司在加利福尼亚州的工伤赔偿保险业务。在此之前，该业务"简直是一场灾难"。Frank 将业务量削减至一年半前的约 25%，并开始取得良好进展。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Frank DeNardo joined Berkshire in the spring of 1978 to straighten out National Indemnity's California Worker's Compensation business, which had been "a disaster." Frank reduced volume to about 25% of what it was eighteen months earlier and began making good progress.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特认为 Frank 具备纠正洛杉矶办公室重大问题所需的"经验和才智"，将他派去处理一个棘手的烂摊子。这体现了巴菲特善于识别问题并找到合适的人来解决的管理风格。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett believed Frank had the "experience and intellect" needed to correct the major problems of the Los Angeles office, sending him to handle a difficult turnaround. This reflects Buffett's management style of identifying problems and finding the right person to solve them.
