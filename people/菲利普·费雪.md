# 菲利普·费雪 / Philip A. Fisher

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 菲利普·费雪（Philip A. Fisher，1907-2004）是美国著名的投资家和作家，被誉为"成长股投资之父"。他的代表作《怎样选择成长股》（*Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits*，1958）是投资经典。巴菲特在 1979 年股东信中首次引用了费雪关于"公司吸引股东如同餐厅吸引顾客"的比喻，用以阐述伯克希尔的股东群体理念。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Philip A. Fisher (1907-2004) was a renowned American investor and author, known as the "father of growth investing." His classic work *Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits* (1958) is an investment staple. Buffett first cited Fisher's restaurant analogy in the 1979 letter to articulate Berkshire's philosophy on shareholder constituency.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 巴菲特在 1979 年信中称费雪为"受人尊敬的投资家和作家"
> - 引用费雪的餐厅比喻来解释伯克希尔为何追求低换手率的股东群体
> - 费雪的投资理念（集中持有优秀成长股）与巴菲特后期的投资风格高度一致
> - 巴菲特曾说自己 85% 像格雷厄姆、15% 像费雪（后逐渐向费雪靠拢）

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Buffett described Fisher as "a respected investor and author" in the 1979 letter
> - Cited Fisher's restaurant analogy to explain why Berkshire seeks low-turnover shareholder base
> - Fisher's philosophy (concentrated holdings in outstanding growth companies) closely aligns with Buffett's later investment style
> - Buffett famously said he was 85% Graham and 15% Fisher (later shifting toward Fisher)

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [1979-letter](../letters/1979-letter.md)
> 受人尊敬的投资家和作家菲利普·费雪曾把一家公司吸引股东的策略比作一家餐厅吸引顾客。一家餐厅可以锁定特定的客户群并最终培养出一群忠实的回头客。但一家餐厅不能三天两头换风格，却指望拥有一批满意而稳定的顾客。
>
> Phil Fisher, a respected investor and author, once likened the policies of the corporation in attracting shareholders to those of a restaurant attracting potential customers. A restaurant could seek a given clientele and eventually obtain an appropriate group of devotees. But the restaurant could not change its character constantly and end up with a happy and stable clientele.
