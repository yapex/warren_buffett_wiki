# 路易·辛普森 / Lou Simpson

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 路易·辛普森（Lou Simpson）是[盖可保险](../companies/盖可保险.md)的投资经理，负责管理GEICO保险业务产生的浮存金的投资。巴菲特称赞他"身上罕见地同时具备了创造出色长期投资业绩所需的气质和智力"，"以低于平均水平的风险，他创造了迄今为止保险业中最好的投资回报"。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Lou Simpson is the investment manager at GEICO, responsible for investing the float generated by GEICO's insurance operations. Buffett praised him for having "the rare combination of temperamental and intellectual characteristics that produce outstanding long-term investment performance", achieving "returns that have been by far the best in the insurance industry" while "operating with below-average risk".
