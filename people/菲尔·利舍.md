# 菲尔·利舍 / Phil Liesche

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 菲尔·利舍（Phil Liesche）是[国民保险公司](../companies/国民保险公司.md)的传奇经理人，负责传统的汽车和一般责任险业务。在 1974-75 年保险业危机后竞争对手大量退出之际，他带领团队实现了保费大幅增长和优异的承保利润率，被巴菲特在 1977 年信中特别表彰。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Phil Liesche is a legendary manager at National Indemnity Company, responsible for traditional auto and general liability business. After many competitors withdrew during the 1974-75 insurance crisis, he led the team to achieve significant premium growth combined with excellent underwriting margins, specifically commended by Buffett in the 1977 letter.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 巴菲特高度信任其承保能力，称其取得"非凡的承保成绩"
> - 被视为伯克希尔保险业务成功的关键人物之一
> - 在承保部门的罗兰·米勒和理赔部门的比尔·莱恩斯协助下工作

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Buffett highly trusted his underwriting ability, calling his achievement "extraordinary"
> - Considered one of the key figures behind Berkshire's insurance success
> - Worked with Roland Miller (Underwriting) and Bill Lyons (Claims)
