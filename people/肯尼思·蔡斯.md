# 肯尼思·蔡斯 / Ken Chace

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 肯尼思·蔡斯（Ken Chace）是伯克希尔纺织业务的管理负责人。在 1965 年公司控制权更迭后，他带领纺织部门运营，尽管行业持续低迷，他的努力使纺织部门产出的资金得以被调配到利润更高的保险业务收购与扩张上，为伯克希尔的转型做出了重要贡献。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Ken Chace managed Berkshire's textile operations. After the change in corporate control in 1965, he led the textile division. Despite persistent industry headwinds, his efforts generated capital from the textile division that was deployed to finance the acquisition and expansion of the profitable insurance operation, making an important contribution to Berkshire's transformation.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 1965 年巴菲特接管后继续管理纺织业务
> - 巴菲特认可其"积极和坦诚"的管理风格
> - 纺织部门在其管理下产出的资金成为保险业务扩张的"弹药"

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Continued managing textiles after Buffett took control in 1965
> - Buffett acknowledged his "energetic and straightforward" management approach
> - Capital generated under his management funded insurance business expansion
