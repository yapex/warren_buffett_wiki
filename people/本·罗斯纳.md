# 本·罗斯纳 / Ben Rosner

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 本·罗斯纳（Ben Rosner）是联合零售商店公司（Associated Retail Stores, Inc.）的联合创始人之一。1931 年与利奥·西蒙（Leo Simon）以一家店面和 3,200 美元在芝加哥创立了这家女装连锁企业。Simon 先生去世后，公司于 1967 年出售给多元零售，Ben 留任继续经营。1978 年与多元零售合并后，联合零售进入伯克希尔体系。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Ben Rosner was co-founder of Associated Retail Stores, Inc. He started the women's apparel chain with Leo Simon in Chicago in 1931 with one store and $3,200. After Mr. Simon's death, the business was sold to Diversified Retailing in 1967, with Ben staying on to run it. The business entered the Berkshire system through the 1978 Diversified merger.

## 与巴菲特的关系 / Relationship with Buffett

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 巴菲特高度赞赏本·罗斯纳的管理才能，特别是在不利行业趋势下仍能实现约 20% 税后资本回报率的能力。巴菲特将 76 岁的 Ben Rosner 与 82 岁的吉恩·阿贝格和 74 岁的路易斯·文森蒂并列，称赞他们每天依然带着近乎狂热的主人翁精神投入工作"。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Buffett highly praised Ben Rosner's management skills, particularly his ability to achieve approximately 20% after-tax returns on capital despite adverse industry trends. Buffett grouped 75-year-old Ben with 81-year-old Gene Abegg and 73-year-old Louie Vincenti, noting they all "continue daily to bring an almost passionately proprietary attitude to the business."
