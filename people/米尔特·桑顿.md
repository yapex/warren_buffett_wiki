# 米尔特·桑顿 / Milt Thornton

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 米尔特·桑顿（Milt Thornton）是赛普勒斯保险公司（Cypress Insurance Company）的管理者，该公司作为伯克希尔的工伤赔偿保险子公司，在 1978 年交出了出色成绩单。巴菲特认为他拥有一支"谨慎而极其专业"的团队来应对工伤赔偿险中通胀与社会观念变化带来的挑战。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Milt Thornton managed Cypress Insurance Company, Berkshire's worker's compensation subsidiary, which turned in outstanding results in 1978. Buffett noted he had a "cautious and highly professional staff" to cope with the challenges of inflation and changing social concepts in the worker's compensation line.
