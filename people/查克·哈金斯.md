# 查克·哈金斯 / Chuck Huggins

## 人物简介 / Biography

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 查克·哈金斯（Chuck Huggins）是[喜诗糖果](../companies/喜诗糖果.md)的总经理，自 1972 年蓝筹印花收购喜诗以来一直管理该公司。在他的领导下，喜诗糖果在几乎不追加资本投入的情况下，税前利润从 420 万美元增长到 1260 万美元（1972-1977），后来更成为年产量超 2700 万磅的糖果帝国。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Chuck Huggins is the president of See's Candies, managing the company since Blue Chip Stamps' acquisition in 1972. Under his leadership, See's pre-tax earnings grew from $4.2M to $12.6M (1972-1977) with virtually no additional capital, later building it into an empire producing over 27 million pounds annually.
