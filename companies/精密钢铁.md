# 精密钢铁 / Precision Steel

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 精密钢铁公司（Precision Steel）是韦斯科金融公司的子公司，从事钢铁加工和分销业务。伯克希尔通过蓝筹印花间接持有其权益。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Precision Steel was a subsidiary of Wesco Financial Corporation, engaged in steel processing and distribution. Berkshire held an indirect interest through Blue Chip Stamps.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **持股路径**：伯克希尔 → 蓝筹印花(60%) → 韦斯科金融(80%) → 精密钢铁
> - **1979 年首次出现在收益表中**：税前利润 $3,254 千（伯克希尔份额 $1,480 千）
> - **1979 年税后利润（伯克希尔份额）**：$723 千

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Ownership Path**: Berkshire → Blue Chip Stamps (60%) → Wesco Financial (80%) → Precision Steel
> - **First appearance in earnings table**: 1979 pre-tax earnings $3,254K (Berkshire share $1,480K)
> - **1979 After-tax Earnings (Berkshire share)**: $723K
