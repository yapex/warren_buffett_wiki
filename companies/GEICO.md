# GEICO

## 公司简介 / Company Profile

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> GEICO（政府雇员保险公司）是美国最大的汽车保险公司之一，以其低成本的直接销售模式著称。伯克希尔持有其约35%的股权（尽管由于投票权委托，会计上按持股20%以下处理）。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> GEICO (Government Employees Insurance Company) is one of the largest auto insurers in the United States, known for its low-cost direct sales model. Berkshire owns approximately 35% of GEICO (though accounting treats it as less than 20% due to delegated voting rights).

## 伯克希尔的投资 / Berkshire's Investment

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1982年，伯克希尔持有GEICO 720万股，市值3.096亿美元，成本仅为4,714万美元。这是伯克希尔最大的未实现收益来源之一。巴菲特在20岁时就与GEICO建立了商业联系，这是一段跨越25年后在1970年代中期重新回归的投资。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> In 1982, Berkshire held 7.2 million shares of GEICO with a market value of $309.6 million at a cost of only $47.14 million. This was one of Berkshire's largest sources of unrealized gains. Buffett first connected with GEICO at age 20, returning as an investor in the mid-1970s after straying for roughly 25 years.

## 管理团队 / Management

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 杰克·伯恩 (Jack Byrne) 在1976年公司濒临破产时拯救了GEICO，展现了卓越的管理才能。比尔·斯奈德 (Bill Snyder) 与伯恩共同管理公司，实现了"让事情保持简单，并牢记你最初的使命"这一最难以捉摸的目标。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Jack Byrne rescued GEICO from bankruptcy in 1976 with exceptional managerial brilliance. Bill Snyder co-manages the company, achieving the most elusive of human goals - keeping things simple and remembering what you set out to do.

## 竞争优势 / Competitive Advantage

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> GEICO是巴菲特所描述的那种"高利润例外"——一家拥有既宽又深的可持续成本优势的公司。路易·辛普森 (Lou Simpson) 是财产意外险行业中最优秀的投资经理。GEICO约2.5亿美元的保费量远超伯克希尔自己保险公司直接承保的全部保费。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> GEICO is a "magnificent illustration of the high-profit exception" - a company with a wide and sustainable cost advantage. Lou Simpson is the best investment manager in the property-casualty business. Berkshire's 35% interest in GEICO represents about $250 million of premium volume, considerably greater than all of the direct volume Berkshire produces.
