# 骑士报业公司 / Knight-Ridder Newspapers, Inc.

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 骑士报业公司（Knight-Ridder Newspapers, Inc.）是美国当时最大的报业集团之一。伯克希尔在 1978 年持有其 453,800 股股票，是保险投资组合中媒体行业的重要持仓。巴菲特对报业情有独钟，因其具备特许经营权特征。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Knight-Ridder Newspapers, Inc. was one of the largest newspaper chains in the United States. Berkshire held 453,800 shares in 1978, making it a significant media holding in the insurance portfolio. Buffett had a strong affinity for newspapers due to their franchise-like characteristics.
