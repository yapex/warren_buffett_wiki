# 联合出版公司 / Affiliated Publications, Inc.

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 联合出版公司（Affiliated Publications, Inc.）是美国一家出版公司，旗下拥有《波士顿环球报》等媒体资产。伯克希尔在 1979 年持有其 289,700 股股票，作为保险投资组合的一部分。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Affiliated Publications, Inc. was an American publishing company owning media assets including The Boston Globe. Berkshire held 289,700 shares in 1979 as part of its insurance portfolio.
