# 英特帕布利克集团 / Interpublic Group of Companies

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 英特帕布利克集团（Interpublic Group of Companies, Inc.）是一家大型广告和营销服务公司。伯克希尔在 1978 年持有其 592,650 股股票，是保险投资组合中增值幅度较大的持仓之一（成本 $4.5M，市值 $19.0M）。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Interpublic Group of Companies, Inc. is a large advertising and marketing services company. Berkshire held 592,650 shares in 1978, representing one of the more significantly appreciated positions in the insurance portfolio (cost $4.5M, market value $19.0M).
