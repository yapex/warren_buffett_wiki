# 蓝筹印花 / Blue Chip Stamps

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 蓝筹印花公司（Blue Chip Stamps）是一家美国印花兑换公司，消费者在零售店购物时可获得印花，集满后可兑换商品。随着印花行业衰落，蓝筹印花逐渐转型为投资控股公司，旗下持有喜诗糖果、韦斯科金融等优质资产。伯克希尔通过多年逐步增持，最终在 1983 年完成合并。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Blue Chip Stamps was an American trading stamp company where consumers received stamps at retail stores and redeemed them for merchandise. As the trading stamp industry declined, Blue Chip Stamps gradually transformed into an investment holding company owning premium assets including See's Candies and Wesco Financial. Berkshire increased its stake over the years and eventually merged with it in 1983.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **1977 年底**：伯克希尔持股约 36.5%
> - **1978 年底**：通过与多元零售合并，持股提升至约 58%，需全面合并报表
> - **1979 年底**：伯克希尔持股约 60%
> - 蓝筹印花持有[喜诗糖果](喜诗糖果.md) 99% 和[韦斯科金融公司](韦斯科金融公司.md) 80%
> - 蓝筹印花 1979 年母公司税前利润 $2.4M
> - 蓝筹印花是伯克希尔间接持有喜诗糖果、韦斯科金融和布法罗晚报的通道
> - 伯克希尔后来通过蓝筹印花积累了大量现金，用于后续收购

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Year-end 1978**: Through merger with Diversified Retailing, Berkshire's stake rose to ~58%, requiring full consolidation
> - **Year-end 1979**: Berkshire owned approximately 60%
> - Blue Chip Stamps owned See's Candies (99%) and Wesco Financial (80%)
> - Blue Chip 1979 parent pre-tax earnings $2.4M
> - Blue Chip Stamps served as the channel through which Berkshire indirectly owned See's Candies and Wesco Financial
> - Later accumulated significant cash through Blue Chip Stamps for subsequent acquisitions
