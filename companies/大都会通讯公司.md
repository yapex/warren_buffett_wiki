# 大都会通讯公司 / Capital Cities Communications

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 大都会通讯公司（Capital Cities Communications）是一家美国媒体集团，拥有电视台、广播电台和报纸等资产。以卓越的管理层和高效的资本配置著称。1985 年巴菲特协助大都会通讯以 35 亿美元收购 ABC，随后伯克希尔成为合并后 Capital Cities/ABC 的大股东。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Capital Cities Communications was an American media group owning television stations, radio stations, and newspapers. Known for its exceptional management and efficient capital allocation. In 1985, Buffett helped Capital Cities acquire ABC for $3.5 billion, and Berkshire became the largest shareholder of the merged Capital Cities/ABC.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **1977 年**：伯克希尔投资 $10.9M 买入 220,000 股，年底市值 $13.2M
> - 巴菲特高度评价其管理层："同时拥有非凡的资产和非凡的管理层"
> - 直接收购其资产需花费约两倍成本，但控制权不会带来更好的管理
> - "不控制反而能获得比控制更好的管理效果"——这一非正统观点的经典案例

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **1977**: Berkshire invested $10.9M for 220,000 shares, year-end market $13.2M
> - Buffett highly praised management: "possesses both extraordinary properties and extraordinary management"
> - Direct acquisition would cost about twice the stock market price, but control would not improve management
> - "Better management result through non-control than control" — a classic case of this unorthodox view

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [1977-letter](../letters/1977-letter.md)
> 大都会同时拥有非凡的资产和非凡的管理层。如果直接收购大都会旗下这样的资产，花费大约是我们通过股票市场购入成本的两倍。实际上，不控制反而能获得比控制更好的管理效果。这是一个非正统的观点，但我们认为是正确的。
>
> Capital Cities possesses both extraordinary properties and extraordinary management. To purchase directly would cost about twice our cost via the stock market. In effect, we can obtain a better management result through non-control than control. This is an unorthodox view, but one we believe to be sound.
