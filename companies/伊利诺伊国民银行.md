# 伊利诺伊国民银行 / Illinois National Bank

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 伊利诺伊国民银行（位于伊利诺伊州罗克福德市）是伯克希尔·哈撒韦的子公司，由吉恩·阿贝格于 1931 年以 25 万美元创立。该银行以远超同行的资产回报率著称，在巴菲特 1969 年收购后持续创造卓越业绩，同时支付了大量现金股利。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> The Illinois National Bank (of Rockford, Illinois) was a Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary, founded by Gene Abegg in 1931 with $250,000. The bank was known for achieving asset returns roughly three times those of most large banks. After Buffett's 1969 acquisition, it continued to produce outstanding results while paying substantial cash dividends.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **1969 年**：伯克希尔收购伊利诺伊国民银行
> - **1977 年**：收益 $3.6M，超过许多规模为其 2-3 倍的银行
> - 资产回报率约为大型银行的 3 倍
> - 自 1969 年收购以来累计支付现金股利 $20M，未增资一分钱
> - 1977 年底吉恩·阿贝格（80 岁）引入彼得·杰弗里作为接班人
> - 后因银行控股公司法案被迫出售（1980 年）

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **1969**: Berkshire acquired the Illinois National Bank
> - **1977**: Earnings $3.6M, exceeding many banks two or three times its size
> - Return on assets about three times that of most large banks
> - Paid $20M in cumulative cash dividends since 1969 acquisition, with no additional capital
> - Late 1977: Gene Abegg (age 80) brought in Peter Jeffrey as successor
> - Later forced to sell due to Bank Holding Company Act (1980)

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [[1977-letter]]
> 吉恩·阿贝格于1931年以25万美元创建了这家银行。从那以后，这家银行没有进行过任何增资；恰恰相反，自从我们1969年买下它以来，已经向我们支付了2,000万美元的现金股利。1977年的收益达到360万美元，超过了许多规模为其两三倍的银行。
>
> Gene Abegg formed the bank in 1931 with $250,000. Since that time, no new capital has been contributed; on the contrary, since our purchase in 1969, dividends of $20 million have been paid. Earnings in 1977 amounted to $3.6 million, more than achieved by many banks two or three times its size.
