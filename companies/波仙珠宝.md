# 波仙珠宝 / Borsheim's

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 公司简介
波仙珠宝（Borsheim's）是一家位于奥马哈的珠宝店，以其巨大的单店规模和极低的运营成本著称，是全美最大的独立珠宝店之一。1989年初被伯克希尔收购。

### 伯克希尔的关系
- 1989年初完成收购，加入"The Sainted Seven Plus One"集团
- 收购第一年即超出所有预期：销售显著增长
- 没有经过审计即完成收购，所有"惊喜"都在正面

### 成功公式
波仙珠宝与内布拉斯加家具商场遵循完全相同的成功公式：
1. 在一个地点拥有无与伦比的商品深度和广度
2. 业内最低的运营成本（费用率约为同行的1/3）
3. 由巨大采购量支撑的精明采购
4. 远低于竞争对手的毛利率和价格
5. 家庭成员全天候在场的友好个性化服务

## 🇺🇸 English

### Company Overview
Borsheim's is an Omaha-based jewelry store known for enormous single-location scale and extremely low operating costs. Acquired by Berkshire in early 1989.

### Berkshire's Relationship
- Acquired early 1989, joining "The Sainted Seven Plus One"
- Exceeded all expectations in first year
- Purchased without audit; all surprises were positive

### Success Formula
Same as Nebraska Furniture Mart: (1) unparalleled depth/breadth at one location, (2) lowest operating costs (~1/3 of industry), (3) shrewdest buying via volume, (4) far lower margins/prices, (5) friendly personalized service with family on hand

### Buffett Quotes
> "If you don't know jewelry, know your jeweler makes sense whether you are buying the whole business or a tiny diamond."
> — [[1989-letter]]
