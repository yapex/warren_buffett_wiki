# 伍尔沃斯 / F. W. Woolworth Company

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 伍尔沃斯公司（F. W. Woolworth Company）是美国著名的零售连锁企业，以"五分一角店"起家。伯克希尔在 1979 年持有其 771,900 股股票，作为保险投资组合的一部分。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> F. W. Woolworth Company was a famous American retail chain that started as "five-and-dime" stores. Berkshire held 771,900 shares in 1979 as part of its insurance portfolio.
