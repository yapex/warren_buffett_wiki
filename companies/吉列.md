# 吉列 / Gillette

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 公司简介
吉列公司是全球领先的剃须用品制造商。巴菲特称其业务"正是我们喜欢的那种"。

### 伯克希尔的关系
- 1989年7月买入6亿美元可转换优先股
- 条款：8.75%股息、10年强制赎回、可按50美元/股转换为普通股
- 巴菲特加入吉列董事会
- 巴菲特认为能理解吉列的商业模式，对其未来有合理判断
- 巴菲特亲自代言Sensor剃须刀："如果你还没试过吉列的新Sensor剃须刀，赶紧去买一个"

### 巴菲特原话精选
> "吉列的业务非常像我们喜欢的那种。查理和我认为我们理解这家公司的经济特征，因此相信我们能对其未来做出合理的智能判断。"
> — [[1989-letter]]

## 🇺🇸 English

### Company Overview
Gillette is a global leader in shaving products. Buffett calls its business "very much the kind we like."

### Berkshire's Relationship
- July 1989: purchased $600M of convertible preferred (8.75% dividend, 10-year mandatory redemption, convertible at $50/share)
- Buffett joined Gillette's board
- "If you haven't tried Gillette's new Sensor razor, go right out and get one."

### Buffett Quotes
> "Gillette's business is very much the kind we like. Charlie and I think we understand the company's economics and therefore believe we can make a reasonably intelligent guess about its future."
> — [[1989-letter]]
