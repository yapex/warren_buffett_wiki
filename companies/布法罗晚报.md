# 布法罗晚报 / Buffalo Evening News

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 布法罗晚报（Buffalo Evening News）是纽约州布法罗市的一家报纸，通过蓝筹印花被纳入伯克希尔体系。1978 年该报仍处于亏损状态，税前亏损 $2.9M，但巴菲特继续持有并支持其发展。后来该报成为伯克希尔媒体投资组合中的重要一员。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> The Buffalo Evening News is a newspaper in Buffalo, New York, brought into the Berkshire system through Blue Chip Stamps. In 1978 the paper was still loss-making with a pre-tax loss of $2.9M, but Buffett continued to hold and support it. It later became an important part of Berkshire's media portfolio.
