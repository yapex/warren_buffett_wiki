# 盖可保险 / GEICO

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 政府员工保险公司（Government Employees Insurance Company，简称 GEICO）是美国最大的直销汽车保险公司之一。以低成本运营模式著称，直接向投保人销售保单，省去代理人佣金，从而提供具有竞争力的费率。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> The Government Employees Insurance Company (GEICO) is one of the largest direct-selling auto insurers in the United States. Known for its low-cost operating model, GEICO sells policies directly to policyholders, eliminating agent commissions and thereby offering competitive rates.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **1976-1977 年**：伯克希尔通过保险子公司开始大量买入 GEICO 股票
> - **1977 年底持仓**：可转换优先股 1,986,953 股（成本 $19.4M，市值 $33.0M）+ 普通股 1,294,308 股（成本 $4.1M，市值 $10.5M）
> - **1978 年底持仓**：可转换优先股市值 $28.3M + 普通股市值 $9.1M = 合计 $37.4M（成本 $23.5M）
> - 持仓总市值 $43.5M（1977）→ $37.4M（1978），因市场整体下跌市值有所回落
> - 这是巴菲特早期最重要的投资之一，后来 GEICO 成为伯克希尔的全资子公司

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **1976-1977**: Berkshire began accumulating GEICO shares through its insurance subsidiaries
> - **Year-end 1977 holdings**: 1,986,953 convertible preferred shares (cost $19.4M, market $33.0M) + 1,294,308 common shares (cost $4.1M, market $10.5M)
> - Total market value $43.5M against cost $23.5M, approximately 85% unrealized gain
> - One of Buffett's most important early investments; GEICO later became a wholly-owned Berkshire subsidiary
