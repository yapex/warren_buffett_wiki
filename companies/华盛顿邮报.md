# 华盛顿邮报 / The Washington Post Company

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 华盛顿邮报公司是美国著名的媒体集团，旗下拥有《华盛顿邮报》报纸、电视台、杂志和教育出版等业务。在巴菲特的投资生涯中，华盛顿邮报是最经典的投资案例之一，也是其"以大幅折扣买入优质企业部分股权"理念的最佳例证。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> The Washington Post Company is a prominent American media conglomerate, owning The Washington Post newspaper, television stations, magazines, and educational publishing businesses. In Buffett's investment career, The Washington Post is one of the most classic cases, exemplifying his philosophy of buying portions of outstanding businesses at large discounts.

> - **1978 年底持仓**：934,300 股，成本 $10.6M，市值 $43.4M，浮盈约 309%
