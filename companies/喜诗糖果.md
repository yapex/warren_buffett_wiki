# 喜诗糖果 / See's Candies

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 喜诗糖果是伯克希尔旗下著名的糖果制造商和零售商，拥有 225 家门店，年产量超 2700 万磅。1972 年由[[蓝筹印花]]收购，是巴菲特从"价格便宜的一般公司"转向"价格合理的好公司"投资理念的关键转折点。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> See's Candies is Berkshire's iconic candy manufacturer and retailer with 225 shops and annual production of over 27 million pounds. Purchased by Blue Chip Stamps in 1972, it was the key turning point in Buffett's shift from buying "cheap average companies" to "good companies at fair prices."

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 1972 年初由[[蓝筹印花]]以约 2500 万美元收购（99% 持股）
> - 1977 年税前经营收益增长至 1260 万美元（1972 年为 420 万美元），几乎没有追加资本投入
> - 属于"The Sainted Seven Plus One"集团
> - 由[[查克·哈金斯]]管理

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Purchased by Blue Chip Stamps for approximately $25 million in early 1972 (99% ownership)
> - Pre-tax operating earnings grew to $12.6M by 1977 (from $4.2M in 1972) with little additional capital
> - Part of "The Sainted Seven Plus One" group
> - Managed by Chuck Huggins

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes
