# 喜诗糖果 / See's Candies

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 公司简介
喜诗糖果是伯克希尔旗下著名的糖果制造商和零售商，拥有225家门店，年产量超2700万磅。

### 伯克希尔的关系
- 属于"The Sainted Seven Plus One"集团
- 1989年销量增长8%，即使在1988年本身是创纪录年份的情况下
- 首次在多年后实现同店销量增长

### 1989年亮点
- 广告支出从400万美元增至500万美元
- 报纸广告支出增长两倍多，达到巴菲特记忆中占销售额的最高比例
- 营销代理商Hal Riney & Partners的广告文案"100%到位"
- 查克·哈金斯（Chuck Huggins）日常参与生产和销售的各个方面

### 巴菲特原话精选
> "喜诗的卓越表现已成为常规。但查克·哈金斯的管理绝不常规：他对生产和销售各方面的日常参与，向我们需要生产和分销超过2700万磅糖果的数千名员工传达了品质和服务的信息。"
> — [[1989-letter]]

## 🇺🇸 English

### Company Overview
See's Candies is Berkshire's iconic candy manufacturer and retailer with 225 shops and annual production of over 27 million pounds.

### Berkshire's Relationship
- Part of "The Sainted Seven Plus One" group
- 8% increase in pounds sold in 1989, despite 1988 being a record year
- First same-store poundage gains in many years

### 1989 Highlights
- Advertising increased from $4M to $5M; newspaper ad spend more than tripled
- Chuck Huggins' daily involvement with all aspects of production and sales

### Buffett Quotes
> "See's splendid performances have become routine. But there is nothing routine about the management of Chuck Huggins."
> — [[1989-letter]]
