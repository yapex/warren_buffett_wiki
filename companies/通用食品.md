# 通用食品 / General Foods, Inc.

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 通用食品公司（General Foods, Inc.）是美国大型食品加工企业，旗下拥有麦斯威尔咖啡、果珍等知名品牌。伯克希尔在 1979 年持有其 328,700 股股票，是保险投资组合中少数市值低于成本的持仓之一。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> General Foods, Inc. was a major American food processing company owning brands such as Maxwell House coffee and Tang. Berkshire held 328,700 shares in 1979, one of the few positions in the insurance portfolio trading below cost.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **持股性质**：少数股权（保险投资组合）
> - **持股数量**：328,700 股（1979年）
> - **买入成本**：$11,437 千
> - **1979 年底市值**：$11,053 千（低于成本）

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Relationship**: Minority equity holding (insurance portfolio)
> - **Shares**: 328,700 (1979)
> - **Cost**: $11.4M
> - **Market Value (Dec 1979)**: $11.1M (below cost)

## 1984年重大发展

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1984年，伯克希尔与通用食品进行了一笔按比例赎回交易：通用食品在公开市场回购股票时，伯克希尔同步出售股份以保持持股比例不变。伯克希尔总共收到21,843,601美元现金，持股比例保持在8.75%不变。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> In 1984, Berkshire executed a proportional redemption with General Foods: as GF repurchased shares in the open market, Berkshire sold shares to maintain its ownership percentage. Berkshire received $21,843,601 in cash while keeping its 8.75% stake unchanged.
