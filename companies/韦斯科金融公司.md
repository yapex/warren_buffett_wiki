# 韦斯科金融公司 / Wesco Financial Corporation

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 韦斯科金融公司是一家多元化的金融服务公司，由蓝筹印花持股 80%。查理·芒格曾担任其董事长。韦斯科旗下包括储蓄贷款、保险等业务，后来成为伯克希尔的子公司。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Wesco Financial Corporation was a diversified financial services company, 80% owned by Blue Chip Stamps. Charlie Munger served as its chairman. Wesco's operations included savings & loan, insurance, and other businesses, later becoming a Berkshire subsidiary.
