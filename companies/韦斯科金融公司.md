# 韦斯科金融公司 / Wesco Financial Corporation

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 韦斯科金融公司是一家多元化的金融服务公司，由蓝筹印花持股 80%。查理·芒格曾担任其董事长。韦斯科旗下包括储蓄贷款、保险等业务，后来成为伯克希尔的子公司。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Wesco Financial Corporation was a diversified financial services company, 80% owned by Blue Chip Stamps. Charlie Munger served as its chairman. Wesco's operations included savings & loan, insurance, and other businesses, later becoming a Berkshire subsidiary.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - 通过[蓝筹印花](蓝筹印花.md)间接持有（80%），伯克希尔间接权益约 46%
> - 由[路易斯·文森蒂](../people/路易斯·文森蒂.md)管理，73 岁，每天仍充满主人翁精神
> - 1978 年母公司税前利润 $1.8M，旗下互助储贷公司税前利润 $10.6M

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - Indirectly held through Blue Chip Stamps (80%), Berkshire's indirect equity ~46%
> - Managed by Louis Vincenti, age 73, still brings "passionately proprietary attitude"
> - 1978 parent pre-tax earnings $1.8M; subsidiary Mutual Savings & Loan $10.6M
