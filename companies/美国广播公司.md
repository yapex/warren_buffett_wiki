# 美国广播公司 / American Broadcasting Companies (ABC)

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 美国广播公司（American Broadcasting Companies, Inc.，简称 ABC）是美国三大广播电视网之一。伯克希尔在 1978 年持有其 246,450 股股票，作为保险投资组合的一部分。ABC 后来与大都会通讯公司合并，成为大都会-美国广播公司（Capital Cities/ABC），最终被迪士尼收购。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> American Broadcasting Companies, Inc. (ABC) was one of the three major U.S. television networks. Berkshire held 246,450 shares in 1978 as part of its insurance portfolio. ABC later merged with Capital Cities to form Capital Cities/ABC, which was eventually acquired by Disney.
