# Cypress保险 / Cypress Insurance Company

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> Cypress 保险公司（Cypress Insurance Company）是伯克希尔旗下的工伤赔偿保险子公司，位于加利福尼亚州。巴菲特称收购 Cypress "事后证明是一颗绝对的明珠"。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Cypress Insurance Company is a Berkshire workers' compensation insurance subsidiary based in California. Buffett called the Cypress acquisition "an absolute gem" in retrospect.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **管理者**：[米尔特·桑顿](../people/米尔特·桑顿.md) (Milt Thornton)
> - **业务特点**：只做自己懂的、想做的业务，不考虑对保费规模的影响
> - **1979 年表现**：工伤赔偿保险表现远远超出合理预期
> - 巴菲特承认在收购方面犯过错误，但称 Cypress 是"绝对的明珠"
> - 拥有一流的业务记录和运转极为顺畅的团队

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Manager**: Milt Thornton
> - **Approach**: Sticks with business he understands and wants, without consideration of volume impact
> - **1979 Performance**: Worker's compensation results far exceeded reasonable expectations
> - Buffett admitted acquisition mistakes but called Cypress "an absolute gem"
> - Outstanding book of business and exceptionally well functioning group

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] [1979-letter](../letters/1979-letter.md)
> 我们承认在收购方面犯过一些错误——而且确实有充分的理由这么说——但收购 Cypress 这笔买卖事后证明是一颗绝对的明珠。
>
> We have admitted — and with good reason — some mistakes on the acquisition front, but the Cypress purchase has turned out to be an absolute gem.
