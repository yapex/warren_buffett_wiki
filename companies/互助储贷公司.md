# 互助储贷公司 / Mutual Savings and Loan Association

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 互助储贷公司（Mutual Savings and Loan Association）是韦斯科金融公司的子公司，从事储蓄贷款业务。伯克希尔通过蓝筹印花间接持有其权益。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Mutual Savings and Loan Association was a subsidiary of Wesco Financial Corporation, engaged in the savings and loan business. Berkshire held an indirect interest through Blue Chip Stamps.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **持股路径**：伯克希尔 → 蓝筹印花(60%) → 韦斯科金融(80%) → 互助储贷
> - **1978 年税前利润**：$10,556 千
> - **1979 年税前利润**：$10,447 千（伯克希尔份额 $4,751 千）
> - **1979 年税后利润（伯克希尔份额）**：$3,261 千

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Ownership Path**: Berkshire → Blue Chip Stamps (60%) → Wesco Financial (80%) → Mutual S&L
> - **1978 Pre-tax Earnings**: $10,556K
> - **1979 Pre-tax Earnings**: $10,447K (Berkshire share $4,751K)
> - **1979 After-tax Earnings (Berkshire share)**: $3,261K
