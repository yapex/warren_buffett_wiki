# 媒体综合 / Media General, Inc.

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 媒体综合公司（Media General, Inc.）是美国一家媒体集团，拥有报纸、电视台和广播电台等资产。伯克希尔在 1979 年持有其 282,500 股股票，作为保险投资组合的一部分。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> Media General, Inc. was an American media group owning newspapers, television stations, and radio stations. Berkshire held 282,500 shares in 1979 as part of its insurance portfolio.
