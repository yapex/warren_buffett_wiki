# General Foods / 通用食品

## 公司简介 / Company Profile

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 通用食品公司 (General Foods Corporation) 是美国主要的食品和饮料公司之一。1982年，伯克希尔持有其约210万股股份，是伯克希尔的四大非控股投资之一。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> General Foods Corporation was a major American food and beverage company. In 1982, Berkshire held approximately 2.1 million shares, making it one of Berkshire's four major non-controlled investments.

## 伯克希尔的投资 / Berkshire's Investment

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 1982年，伯克希尔持有通用食品 2,101,244 股，成本为6,627.7万美元，市值为8,368万美元。通用食品是伯克希尔在1981年年报中预测的"四大非控股公司"之一，1982年其未分配经营收益中伯克希尔应享有的份额超过4,000万美元。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> In 1982, Berkshire held 2,101,244 shares of General Foods at a cost of $66.277 million and market value of $83.68 million. General Foods was one of the "four major non-controlled holdings" predicted in the 1981 annual report, with Berkshire's share of undistributed operating earnings exceeding $40 million in 1982.

## 投资理念 / Investment Philosophy

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 通用食品代表了伯克希尔"未列报的所有者权益收益"投资理念的典型案例。巴菲特认为，这类持股的重要性巨大且持续增长，报告的经营收益数字对伯克希尔的参考意义已相当有限。真正重要的是"经济收益"——包含所有未分配收益，无论持股比例高低。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> General Foods represents a typical case of Berkshire's "Non-Reported Ownership Earnings" investment philosophy. Buffett believes such positions are of very large and growing importance, making reported operating earnings of limited significance. What matters is "economic" earnings - including all undistributed earnings, regardless of ownership percentage.

## 巴菲特原话 / Buffett Quotes

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> "在1981年年报中，我们预测1982年我们在四大非控股公司中应享有的未分配收益将合计超过3,500万美元。今年，其中三家公司——盖可保险、通用食品和华盛顿邮报——的持股比例未变，第四家雷诺工业则大幅增持，这四家公司1982年未分配经营收益中我们应享有的份额远超4,000万美元。"

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> "In our 1981 annual report we predicted that our share of undistributed earnings from four of our major non-controlled holdings would aggregate over $35 million in 1982. With no change in our holdings of three of these companies - GEICO, General Foods and The Washington Post - and a considerable increase in our ownership of the fourth, R. J. Reynolds, our share of undistributed 1982 operating earnings of this group came to well over $40 million."
