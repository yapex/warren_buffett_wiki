# SAFECO公司 / SAFECO Corporation

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> SAFECO 公司（SAFECO Corporation）是一家大型财产意外险公司，被巴菲特誉为"全美经营最好的大型财产意外险公司"。伯克希尔在 1978 年持有其 953,750 股股票，成本低于账面价值。巴菲特以 SAFECO 为核心案例论证了"不控制反而更好"的投资哲学——持有少数股权、被动享受优秀管理层成果，远胜于以溢价收购控制权。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> SAFECO Corporation is a large property and casualty insurance company that Buffett called "probably the best run large property and casualty insurance company in the United States." Berkshire held 953,750 shares in 1978 at a cost below book value. Buffett used SAFECO as the central case study for his "better management through non-control" philosophy — passively owning a minority stake with excellent management is far superior to paying a premium for control.

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **持股性质**：少数股权（保险投资组合）

> - **1978 年对应收益**：约 $6.1M（仅约 18% 以股利形式体现）
> - **1979 年底市值**：$35,527 千

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **Relationship**: Minority equity holding (insurance portfolio)
> - **Shares**: 953,750
> - **Cost**: $23.9M (below book value)
> - **Market Value (Dec 1978)**: $26.5M
> - **Attributable Earnings (1978)**: ~$6.1M (only ~18% reflected as dividends)
> - **Market Value (Dec 1979)**: $35.5M

## 巴菲特原话精选 / Buffett Quotes

> [!quote] 全美最好的大型财险公司
> SAFECO 大概是全美经营最好的大型财产意外险公司。他们的承保能力无与伦比，损失准备金提列保守稳健，投资策略也非常合理。
>
> SAFECO probably is the best run large property and casualty insurance company in the United States. Their underwriting abilities are simply superb, their loss reserving is conservative, and their investment policies make great sense.
> — [1978-letter](../letters/1978-letter.md)

> [!quote] 低于账面价值买入最优公司
> 我们以不到一元的价格买到了行业中最好的公司，而在企业并购交易中，平庸的公司却要付出远超过一元的价格。而要从零开始——前景还不确定——你根本不可能以低于一元的代价做到。
>
> We paid less than 100 cents on the dollar for the best company in the business, when far more than 100 cents on the dollar is being paid for mediocre companies in corporate transactions. And there is no way to start a new operation - with necessarily uncertain prospects - at less than 100 cents on the dollar.
> — [1978-letter](../letters/1978-letter.md)
