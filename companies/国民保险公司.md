# 国民保险公司 / National Indemnity Company

## 公司简介 / Company Overview

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> 国民保险公司（National Indemnity Company）是伯克希尔·哈撒韦保险帝国的基石。1967 年初，巴菲特以约 860 万美元收购了国民保险公司及其姊妹公司国民火灾与海事保险公司，由此进军保险行业。这家公司是伯克希尔保险浮存金模式的起点。

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> National Indemnity Company is the cornerstone of Berkshire Hathaway's insurance empire. In early 1967, Buffett acquired National Indemnity and its sister company National Fire and Marine for approximately $8.6 million, entering the insurance industry. This company was the starting point for Berkshire's insurance float model.

## 伯克希尔的关系 / Berkshire's Relationship

> [!zh] 🇨🇳
> - **1967 年**：以约 $8.6M 收购（收购当年保费收入 $22M）
> - **1977 年**：保险集团总保费收入 $151M（较 1967 年增长 ~600%），全部未增发新股
> - **1978 年**：菲尔·利舍团队保费 ~$90M，承保利润 ~$11M，被誉为"真正非凡的成就"
> - 由[菲尔·利舍](../people/菲尔·利舍.md)负责传统汽车和一般责任险业务，业绩卓越
> - 1974-75 年危机后竞争对手退出，国民保险获得量和利的双重增长
> - 1967 年买下的这块业务至今仍是伯克希尔保险业务中最好的

> [!en] 🇺🇸
> - **1967**: Acquired for ~$8.6M (premium volume $22M that year)
> - **1977**: Total insurance group premium volume $151M (~600% growth since 1967), with no additional shares issued
> - Traditional auto and general liability business managed by Phil Liesche with outstanding results
> - After 1974-75 crisis competitor exits, National Indemnity gained both volume and profitability
